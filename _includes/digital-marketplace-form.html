As part of designing and building the Digital Marketplace, we will be actively engaging with a range of people about their experiences of and ideas about government procurement of digital and technology services. Please register using the form below if you are interested.

A couple of things you need to know:

  • The Digital Marketplace will be an online directory of digital and technology services for government agencies to procure information technology (IT) solutions. More information can be found can be found on the DTA Blog.
  • A prototype version of the marketplace will be available in 2016.
  • While we are improving the user experience of procurement, we are not in charge of day to day government procurement. All questions about ICT related procurement should be directed to the Department of Finance ICT Procurement team or relevant area.
