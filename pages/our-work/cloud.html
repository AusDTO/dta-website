We're creating a new platform to make delivering and operating digital services easier.

  • First services on the platform — February 2016

  • Self-service alpha expected — November/December 2016

cloud.gov.au will make it easier to release, monitor and grow user-facing digital services. This will free up teams to focus on writing code to meet user needs.

We're testing the platform with a small number of services in our delivery hubs. We'll be opening this up to more services later in 2016.
