The DTO believes that everyone who needs to use government services should be able to find what they need to get it done quickly and easily.

We're working with government to make services simpler, clearer and faster for everyone. Here are our current priorities:

  1. Transform services

    We're working with government agencies to help transform services.

  2. Build a beta of GOV.AU

    We're bringing together services and information from across government into one place.

  3. Build an identity alpha

    We're making it easier for people to prove who they are to government online.

  4. Create the Digital Marketplace

    We're transforming how government buys digital and technology services.

  5. Build a performance dashboard

    We're publishing information about the performance of government services on a new dashboard.

  6. Create cloud.gov.au

    We're creating a new platform to make delivering and operating digital services easier.

  7. Support delivery of the Digital Transformation Agenda projects

    We're helping agencies deliver improvements to existing government services.

  8. Create digital communities of practice

    We're creating communities of digital practitioners across the Australian Government.

  9. Continue iterating the Digital Service Standard

    We're setting out the standard for good government digital services.
