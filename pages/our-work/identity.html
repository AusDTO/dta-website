We're making it easier for people to prove who they are when using government services online.

  • Discovery started — January 2016

    • Read the blog post

  • Alpha — an announcement regarding the progress of the project is expected shortly

Users should only need to establish their identity once when using different government services online. We're building an alpha of a service to verify the identity of individuals to a level that means they can access government services.

We're also developing a Trusted Digital Identity Framework (TDIF) to make it easier for the DTO and other government agencies to work together.
